  • You are responsible for archiving the art once I deliver it to you. I routinely clear out my storage.

You will receive .JPG/.JPEG file(s) unless you explicitly state that you require an image with transparency (.PNG). Emotes will be sent as .PNG files. By default, my drawings will have a gray background. You may ask for another flat color in the form if you desire.

If I make an error due to misinterpreting poor references or unclear/incomplete descriptions, I am not required to make corrections. Be clear with your requests and make sure everything is in the price quote I send to you.

  • Please don't waste my time. Do not expect me to re-draw entire scenes for free after you approve of the initial sketch! I will charge you for extensive revisions based on what was completed and what you ask me to draw. For example, if you ask me for a colored sketch scene where two characters are sitting back to back, I doodle them out (you give me the green light to continue working), complete the sketch in its entirety, and then you ask me to draw those same characters in a completely different pose or angle, I will offer to either continue working with the first sketch towards the colored sketch goal, or redraw the whole scene & color it, but you are required to pay for both the initial sketch and for the colored sketch as they will be considered two separate drawings. Small changes, like facial expressions, are fine as long as they happen before I start adding color, and, as long as you don't abuse my good will.

Unless a commission is strictly private (see below), I reserve the right to post a full resolution (albeit watermarked) to a VIP discord channel for my Twitch Subscribers.

  • By commissioning me, you affirm you're at least 18 years of age.
  • All non-human (i.e., furry, splatoon, etc.) characters will be given human proportions.
  • Tips are always welcomed & appreciated uwu
