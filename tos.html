Basics

  • Requests (free art) are not commissions (paid art). I don't take requests. Sorry. Art is a luxury, not a necessity; I am not obligated to accept.
  • If you saw a style in my gallery that you do not see listed here, you are welcome to ask if I would be willing.
  • I expect to be provided with references of the character(s), a brief description of their personality, and at least a general idea of what you want drawn. Designing a character from description invokes a design fee (see below)
  • My art style is subject to change from one piece to the next.
  • You are responsible for archiving the art once I deliver it to you. I routinely clear out my storage.
  • Finished art will be provided via our main source of communication [Discord/Note/DM, etc.] and your PayPal email. You will receive .JPG/.JPEG file(s) unless you explicitly state that you require an image with transparency (.PNG). Emotes will be sent as .PNG files.
  • I reserve the right to artistic freedom & interpretation.
  • I usually provide a WIP at the sketch, flat colors, and background flats stages.
  • You can ask for changes during the WIP stages, however, there are limits. I will charge you for extensive revisions (see below). If I have made an error due to misinterpreting poor references or unclear/incomplete descriptions, I am not required to make corrections. Be clear with your requests and make sure everything is in the price quote I send to you.
  • Sketch stage: corrections for blatant errors/missing items | Flat colors stage: corrections for smaller details (like accessories or incorrect base colors | Background flat stage: corrections for color ONLY.
  • If I'm unsure of what you want, I'll doodle out a stick figure sketch, and if you like it, we'll work from there.
  • Please don't waste my time. Do not expect me to re-draw entire scenes for free after you approve of the initial sketch - I will charge you for extensive revisions based on what was completed and what you ask me to draw. For example, if you ask me for a colored sketch scene where two characters are sitting back to back, I doodle them out (you give me the green light to continue working), complete the sketch in its entirety, and then you ask me to draw those same characters in a completely different pose or angle, I will offer to either continue working with the first sketch towards the colored sketch goal, or redraw the whole scene & color it, but you will need to pay for the initial sketch and for the colored sketch as they will be considered two separate drawings. Small changes, like facial expressions, are fine as long as they happen before I start adding color, and, as long as you don't abuse my good will.
  • Avoid uploading the unwatermarked versions publicly (where anyone may access it) if you don't want them stolen/reposted by randoms. Unless a commission is strictly private (see below), I reserve the right to post a full resolution (albeit watermarked) to a VIP discord channel for my Twitch Subscribers.
  • Do not rush or harass me. Please. I have a life outside of drawing commissions. If you have not heard from me in 7 days, you are welcome to inquire about what's going on.
  • Tips are always welcomed & appreciated uwu
