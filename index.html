Is this commission going to be used commercially?:
Commercial work includes any art that will bring you profit sans emotes/badges made for Twitch/Discord. This applies to individuals and firms alike. Logos, Banners, and other "channel-related artwork" are considered commercial work, as they promote your channel and indirectly bring you income. Printing many copies and selling them brings income as well - that's commercial work. Printing the artwork and hanging it on your wall / gifting it to a friend / etc., does not constitute as commercial work. If you aren't sure, please ask me!

Skip Queue/Rush/Deadline:
I work on one commission at a time with the exception of rush/priority orders.
Add me to queue - you will be added to the end of the queue, and I will contact you as soon as I get to your turn.
Skip queue - you will skip the queue, and I will work on your commission after I finish the commission I'm currently working on. This will not be done ASAP, I will work at my normal speed. The more people you skip, the more expensive it'll be.
Rush/Priority - skip the queue and skip the commission I'm currently working on. I will work on it ASAP, during every waking moment of my day to get your commission done as quickly as physically possible. The more people you skip, the more expensive it'll be.
View my Updated Queue for more details.
If it is your turn in queue and you're not ready, you may opt to be put on hold until you are. The queue includes everyone, but you may opt to disregard the ones on hold when you place your order.
